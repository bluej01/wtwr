Starter Rifle
  • It is necessary for all able-bodied (physically, mentally and morally able) adults to own and know how to use the most common rifle.
  • One of the most effective personal protection tools.
  • Can be used to hunt small game in an emergency.
  • A good affordable starter rifle for those who are new.
  • Must be shipped to an FFL shop to complete the background check and checkout process.
Armor
  • Armor plates are inserted into plate carriers.
  • There are different levels of armor ratings.
Rounds
  • Full metal jacket (FMJ) rounds are preferred for the range.
  • Hollow point (HP) rounds are perferred for personal protection.
Starter Safe
  • Digital keypad and key access.
  • Safes are essential for responsible arm ownership.
  • Helps prevent unauthorized access.
Medkit
  • Emergency essential.
  • 420 piece kit.
  • Portable and organized.
Hearing Protection
  • Protects hearing from loud noises.
  • Still allows you to hear your surroundings.
  • Powered by 2 AAA batteries.
Safety Glasses
  • Scratch, impact, fog, and UV resistant.
  • Adjustable temples and nose pad.
N95 Mask
  • Critical for wildfires or air pollution.
